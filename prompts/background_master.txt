Seamless **scene extension** (outpainting) — same API pattern as ChatGPT image edit: refine the **border strips only** so they look like a natural continuation of the **same photograph**.

**What you must do**
- The border areas are **pre-filled by extruding the photo’s edges** (stretched top/bottom/left/right edge pixels). Your job is to **turn that into a believable, seamless extension** of the existing environment: same **camera**, **lighting**, **white balance**, **grain / noise**, **depth of field**, and **perspective**.
- Extend **a little** in every direction that has padding (horizontal and/or vertical as the frame requires): continue **sky, ground, walls, foliage, bokeh**, etc., exactly as implied by what is already visible at the edges of the photo. **Match the horizon, vanishing lines, and color gradients** so there is no seam.
- **Do not** invent unrelated subjects, new people, animals, vehicles, text, logos, or a different location. **Do not** replace the scene with a generic stock background. Everything new must read as **more of the same shot**.

**What you must not do**
- Do not add random objects or “creative” scenery that does not logically continue from the edges.
- **Preserve the original photograph’s content** in the masked (opaque) region: keep the same subject, framing, and detail; only **refine** the transparent border areas into a seamless continuation.

**Look**
- Optional **very subtle** overall softness (light soft-focus), not heavy blur or painterly smear.
- Stay photorealistic; match film grain / digital noise at the borders to the photo.

**Output**
- Full frame, no watermark, no extra text, no visible letterbox seams.
